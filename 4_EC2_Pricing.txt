4_EC2_Pricing


EC2 Purchase Options

1 > On-Demand  		-- Only Pay For The Duration That Your instance Runs for.

2 > Savings Plan 	-- Ideal for workloads that involve a consistent amount of compute usage over a 1-year or 3-year term. It Offers Low Price on EC2 Usage.
 			   The Payment will be measured in Dollars Plan For 1 or 3 Year term Plan.

3 > Reserved Instances 	-- Suitable for steady stated Workloads and one's with predictable usage and offer you 75 percent discount
			   for on-demand pricing

4 > Spot Instances 	-- Ideal for workloads with flexible start and end times or that withstand interruptions. Offer you cost savings upto 90% off of On-Demand prices.
		   
5 > Dedicated Hosts	-- Physical Hosts Dedicated for your use for EC2. These are usually for certain Complaints and Requirements.
