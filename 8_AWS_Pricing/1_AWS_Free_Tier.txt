1_AWS_Free_Tier


	- The AWS Free Tier enables you to begin using certain services without having to worry about incurring costs for the specified period. 

	- Three types of offers are available: 1> Always Free

					       2> 12 Months Free

					       3> Trials



1> Always Free

	- These Offers do not Expire and are available to all AWS Customers.

	- EX:- AWS Lambda allows 1 million free requests and up to 3.2 million seconds of compute time per month. 
	       Amazon DynamoDB allows 25 GB of free storage per month.



2> 12 Months Free

	- These offers are free for 12 months following your initial sign up date to AWS.

	- EX:- specific amounts of Amazon S3 Standard Storage, thresholds for monthly hours of Amazon EC2 compute time, and amounts of Amazon CloudFront data transfer out.


3> Trails

	- Short-term free trial offers start from the date you activate a particular service.

	- The length of each trial might vary by number of days or the amount of usage in the service.

	- EX:- Amazon Inspector offers a 90-day free trial. Amazon Lightsail (a service that enables you to run virtual private servers) offers 750 free hours of usage over a 30-day period.
